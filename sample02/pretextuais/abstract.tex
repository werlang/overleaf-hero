\begin{englishabstract}{Previsão de valores de ativos do mercado financeiro com Machine Learning}{Artificial Inteligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Neural Networks, Stock Prediction, Keras}
This work aims to use Machine Learning practices to predict stock values in the financial market using Deep Learning algorithms taking as reference the stock price history during a given period of time, even with the difficulty of predicting values with 100\% accuracy due to share values being affected by factors that can often be unpredictable, it is possible to predict values that come close to the real value of the share based on its price history. Different topology models of artificial neural networks are evaluated in this work, the models LSTM, GRU and TCN performed well in predicting stock price trend.
\end{englishabstract}
